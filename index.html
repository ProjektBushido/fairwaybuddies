Frequently asked questions

Is Fairway Buddies free?

Yes—our Starter plan is free. Premium adds unlimited messaging, prioritizes your position in the Discovery tab, and connect with golfers in any city.

How do you connect with golfers?

You can view golfer cards within the Discover tab and take a peek at their profile. If you want to connect with the golfer, just send them a message request!

What about safety?

We have in‑app report/block tools, community guidelines, and privacy controls. Please review them before your first round.

Which regions do you support?

We’re launching in the U.S. first and expanding from there. Course data improves as more golfers join.
