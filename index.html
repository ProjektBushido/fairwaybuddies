FAQ

Questions before downloading.

Is Fairway Buddies free?

Yes. You can start free with core discovery, community access, and round tracking. Premium unlocks more reach and convenience.

How does Fairway Buddies match golfers?

Golfers can discover others by location, skill level, gender preferences, availability, profile details, and playing style.

Can I use it when traveling?

Yes. Premium users can search any city, which is useful for finding golfers before a trip or weekend getaway.

What are community chat rooms?

They are shared spaces for topics like global golf talk, new golfer questions, premium conversations, tech support, and future regional groups.

Can I track my golf rounds?

Yes. The virtual scorecard lets you log scores, putting, GIR, FIR, notes, and round history.

What safety features are included?

Fairway Buddies supports message requests, privacy controls, report/block options, and community guidelines to help people connect with confidence.
